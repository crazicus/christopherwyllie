About

Hi there!

California born, Utah raised, and Georgia living, I've had a passion for computers since the 6th grade when I bought my first computer so I could play Minecraft. After finding out that it couldn't do what I wanted it to, I delved down the rabbit hole of custom built computers, finally building my first in my junior year of high school, which I still use today. That launched my love of all things digital, and I began learning Java with my best friend Bryce.

Fast forward a few years, and I'm now a sophomore at Emory University, studying Computer Science and German Studies and on track to earn a Bachelors of Science degree in May of 2020. I'm the Dev Initiative chair for HackEmory, Emory University's premiere coding club, and in my free time I write code and dance salsa!

I've also been learning German since my sophomore year of high school, and I'll be studying abroad in the beautiful city of Berlin in the fall, where I'll be pushing my German major nearly to completion, also placing me within reach of the TU-Berlin, a wealth of technical knowledge.

Reach out to me via any of my social media accounts above, or shoot me an email at chris@christopherwyllie.com!
